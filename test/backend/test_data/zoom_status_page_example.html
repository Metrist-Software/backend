Announcing Poly Trio updates for devices provisioned by trio.zoom.us Aug 27, 22:00-23:00 PDT
We will be posting the Poly Trio 7.2.3 update for Trio 8500 and 8800 devices and Poly Trio 7.3.1 update for Trio C60 devices being used as Zoom Rooms Controllers and configured to use trio.zoom.us provisioning server (as described at https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/360000894723-Using-a-Polycom-Trio-as-a-Zoom-Room-Controller).

Trios being used as Zoom Rooms Controllers will automatically poll the trio.zoom.us provisioning server once per day at a random time between the hours of 12:00am - 06:00am in their configured timezone. Once they contact the provisioning server, they will refresh their provisioning information and be instructed to download the update, install and reboot. After rebooting, the Trio will be updated to its new software; the Poly Trio 7.2.3 software for Poly Trio 8500 and 8800 contains the Zoom Rooms Android Controller 5.11.0 application, and the Poly Trio 7.3.1 software for Poly Trio C60 contains the Zoom Rooms Android Controller 5.10.3 application. The Zoom Rooms Android Controller will retain its configuration and automatically pair to its configured Zoom Room.

San Jose Zoom Contact Center Maintenance Sep 9, 23:00 - Sep 10, 23:00 PDT
We will be providing an update to Zoom Contact Center in the San Jose data center.

Zoom Contact Center users registered to the San Jose data center will be automatically registered in an alternative datacenter when the maintenance window starts. During the maintenance window, a brief disconnection may be experienced and notifications may be missed. If you get disconnected, the system will attempt to reconnect you automatically.

If you are a BYOC (Bring Your Own Carrier) customer, please ensure that your Session Border Controller (SBC) is configured with alternate routing to Zoom’s Denver data center in addition to the San Jose data center. During the maintenance window, connectivity to Zoom’s San Jose data center will be blocked which will force calls from the Session Border Controller (SBC) to failover to Zoom’s Denver data center. Traffic will continue to be routed through the Zoom San Jose data center once the maintenance is completed.
